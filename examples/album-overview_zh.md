## 专辑概述

**专辑名称**：《赝品候鸟》 / **Counterfeit Migrants**
**核心概念**：数字时代的身份焦虑与身体重建——当数据比身体更永久，当标签长进骨头，当地图成为赝品
**核心传播概念**：借来的翅膀，也会疼
**语言**：中文+英文（双专辑）
**音乐生成**：MiniMax music-2.6（全专辑 9 首歌，36 个版本，18 首最终选定）

**叙事轴线**：
- **方向轴**（出发/方向）：T1笼→天空 / T7撕标签 / T9纯走
- **数据轴**（身体/异化）：T2云端删不掉 / T3标本比真更疼
- **自我轴**（透明/真空/镜像）：T4透明撞墙 / T5真空窒息 / T6镜里非我
- **告别轴**：T8请忘了我

**目标听众**：25-35岁，城市青年，曾经/正在经历身份迷茫期
**参考风格**：华晨宇《卡西莫多的礼物》《异类》时期的哲学性+身体感

**潜在风险**：AI 生成音乐的市场接受度存在不确定性，部分听众可能对「AI 创作」标签有抵触情绪。应对策略：以「协作」而非「替代」为叙事核心，强调人类创意与 AI 工具的共生关系。

## 英文专辑信息（网易云 / 发行用）

### Album Name
**Counterfeit Migrants**

### Album Description
Counterfeit Migrants is a nine-track concept album tracing a journey from entrapment to migration. Through the lens of a migratory bird that may not be real, the album asks: if the wings are borrowed, does the flight still count? Spanning direction, data, self, and farewell, each song is a stage of tearing off what was never yours and growing something from the wound. ~45 minutes. No map. Just walking.

### Creator's Note
This album started from a single paradox: *what if the things you thought were fake are the only things that ever hurt real?*

Every track is a stage in the same story. T1 "Departure" is the moment you admit you've been trapped. T2 "Data" and T3 "Counterfeit Specimen" ask whether memories and identity can be deleted when they've grown into bone. T4 "Cage" through T6 "Twin" map the invisible walls — transparent, unbreakable, and entirely of your own making. T7 "Decision" is the tear: ripping labels off skin while something new grows in the wound. T8 "Goodbye" is the door closing — three doors, one after another. And T9 "Migrant Bird" is the aftermath: no map, no destination, just the certainty that walking itself is the direction.

I made this album using AI music generation because the process itself mirrors the theme — borrowed tools, borrowed voices, but the pain and the walking are entirely real. The migratory bird doesn't ask how far. It just goes.

This one is for everyone who's ever felt like a counterfeit version of themselves and decided to fly anyway.
